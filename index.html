In conversation with Jeremy Arthur

An interview by Sukhninder Singh

Sukh— Why did you choose graphic design ?

Jeremy—Too improve on my over all skill in a certain different styles, mediums etc.

Sukh— What makes you a good graphic designer?

Jeremy— I would like to think I’m still a begginer, but that said I have always been creative and it helps coming up with different designs of one subject.

Sukh—What are your strengths and weaknesses?

Jeremy—My biggest weakness is any art on a computer, my strength is hand done illustrations.

Sukh—What qualities and skills should a great graphic designer have?

Jeremy—I think having an open mind helps dont be afraid to broaden your horizons And patience is a great asset somone may have in this field.

Sukh— Do you work better alone or in a group?

Jeremy—It really depends on who the person/ people are but I would have to say alone.

Sukh— Where do you see yourself in five years?

Jeremy— Haha... probably back in school for something else. If things went my way I would work for a semi/ local design shop or maybe just sell logos to local buinesses.
